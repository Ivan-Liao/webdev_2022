Rules

These are the rules and punishments that are implemented on Tapple, with thorough explanation of each rule. The rules are enforced by the staff members, and they follow the guidelines to carry out the punishments.

Playing on the server means that you agree to follow the rules so that we can protect your experience from illegitimate cheaters and allow your gameplay to be undisturbed.

Punishment times are determined by the severity of the offense and previous offenses that a player has. (Severity 1: least severe, Severity 4: most severe)

If you believe someone is breaking the rules, you can report it here.
